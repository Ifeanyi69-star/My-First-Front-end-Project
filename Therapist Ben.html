THERAPIST BEN

Ben is a certified skincare therapist specializing in Back Detox Treatments for men. His sessions are ideal for those dealing with back acne, oily skin, or muscle tension caused by stress or physical activity. Using a combination of deep cleansing, exfoliation, and detoxifying clay masks, Ben effectively unclogs pores and rejuvenates the skin while promoting muscular relaxation and circulation.

Originally from Makurdi, Benue State, Nigeria, Ben was raised in a close-knit community where natural healing and traditional wellness were a way of life. Inspired by his mother’s use of herbal remedies and his own experience with adolescent skin issues, Ben pursued a career in esthetics with a focus on men's dermatological needs.

Ben has trained at several leading wellness institutes across Nigeria and South Africa. His approach is both scientific and holistic, combining modern skincare innovations with stress-relief techniques to ensure lasting results. Clients consistently praise his calm demeanor, thorough consultations, and visible improvements after just a few sessions.

Outside his practice, Ben enjoys hiking, researching new skincare ingredients, and mentoring young professionals in the grooming industry. He believes that clear skin and physical ease can significantly boost a man’s confidence and overall quality of life — and his treatments reflect that philosophy.

THERAPIST JOHN

John is a skilled athletic massage therapist who works closely with sports clients, fitness enthusiasts, and individuals with active lifestyles. His Sports Massage sessions are designed to improve flexibility, prevent injuries, and accelerate muscle recovery after intense physical activity. With deep knowledge of muscle mechanics, he applies pressure techniques that target fatigue, soreness, and performance-related tension.

A proud native of Warri, Delta State, Nigeria, John’s journey into bodywork started on the football field. As a former amateur athlete, he experienced firsthand the toll intense training could take on the body. Motivated by a desire to help athletes perform better and recover faster, he pursued professional training in sports therapy, kinesiology, and muscular rehabilitation.

With over 8 years of experience, John has worked with local football clubs, gym clients, and marathon runners, tailoring each session to the physical demands of his clients’ routines. He integrates stretching routines, myofascial release, and deep tissue work into his treatments for lasting results.

Outside the clinic, John is passionate about fitness education and often volunteers as a wellness coach in youth development centers. He enjoys jogging at dawn, watching sports documentaries, and giving tips on injury prevention to young athletes. His goal is to ensure that every client, whether a weekend jogger or a full-time athlete, leaves his table feeling stronger, looser, and recharged.

THERAPIST SAM

Sam delivers deeply rejuvenating facials specifically designed for men. His signature Gentlemen’s Facials target common skin concerns such as roughness, oiliness, enlarged pores, and fatigue. With a steady hand and knowledge of male skin physiology, Sam helps clients achieve smoother, clearer, and more energized complexions—without stripping the skin of its natural balance.

He hails from Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria, and was raised in a home where self-presentation and confidence were deeply valued. Sam’s interest in skincare began during his university years, when he helped friends who struggled with acne and razor bumps. Encouraged by their positive results, he pursued formal training in esthetics and dermal care with a focus on men’s skin, which is often underserved in the beauty and spa industry.

Sam is known for his gentle technique, professionalism, and ability to make even the most skincare-shy clients feel comfortable. His facial routines are a perfect blend of exfoliation, deep cleansing, and calming masks—tailored to suit each client’s skin type and lifestyle. From busy executives to young professionals, his clientele appreciates his ability to refresh their look and boost their confidence.

Outside his spa treatments, Sam is a lifestyle enthusiast who enjoys reading about skincare science, working out, and mentoring young men about grooming and confidence. He believes skincare is not just about appearance, but self-respect—and his work reflects that belief in every session.

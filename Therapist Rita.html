THERAPIST RITA

Therapist Rita specializes in Shiatsu massage, a traditional Japanese technique that uses finger pressure to improve circulation, reduce tension, and promote emotional harmony. With over 8 years of hands-on experience, she artfully integrates modern acupressure with ancient Eastern wisdom to help clients release physical blockages and restore inner balance. Her approach is both holistic and intuitive, often leaving clients with a deep sense of relaxation and rejuvenation.

Rita hails from Oshimili South, Asaba in Delta State. Raised in a humble family, she faced financial struggles early in life, but remained driven by a strong desire to bring healing and comfort to others. Inspired by her grandmother’s herbal remedies and community care, Rita pursued formal training in therapeutic massage and bodywork. Her background taught her resilience, compassion, and the importance of personalized care, which she brings into every session.

Outside her professional work, Rita enjoys quiet meditation, volunteering in wellness outreach programs, and learning about traditional healing from different cultures. Her clients often praise her for her calming presence, gentle demeanor, and ability to understand both physical pain and emotional stress. Whether you're seeking relief from chronic discomfort or simply want a moment of peace, a session with Rita is a healing experience you won’t forget.

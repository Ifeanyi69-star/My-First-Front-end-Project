About Us

Our Story

At Joy's House of Bliss, we believe in the transformative power of touch. With a passion for wellness and a heart for healing, our spa was founded to offer a sanctuary for anyone seeking relief, relaxation, and rejuvenation. Our dedicated team of licensed massage therapists and skincare experts combine age-old techniques with modern practices to provide a personalized, luxurious experience. Whether you're visiting us for a calming massage, a refreshing facial, or a pampering spa session, we’re here to help you rediscover balance and inner peace—one moment at a time.


THE CEO

MISS JOY OKWURUMGBE IFEANYICHUKWU


Born in the heart of Asaba, Delta State, but proudly hailing from Ute-Erumu in Ika North-East Local Government Area, Miss Joy Okwurumgbe Ifeanyichukwu is the visionary behind Joy’s House of Bliss. A passionate wellness advocate and a firm believer in the healing power of rest and beauty, Joy founded the spa with a mission to create a space where physical, emotional, and spiritual calm could be restored through expertly delivered therapeutic care.

With an academic background rooted in International Studies And Diplomacy, Joy blends traditional African skincare wisdom with modern spa innovations. Her journey began with humble roots and a deep commitment to personal growth and helping others feel whole. She leads her team not just as a businesswoman, but as a mentor and nurturer who inspires excellence, elegance, and empathy in service delivery.

Outside her professional role, Miss Joy is known for her warm personality, her love for natural remedies, and her passion for female empowerment through skill training and wellness education. Her leadership style reflects compassion, creativity, and a clear vision to redefine self-care in Nigeria—making luxury wellness experiences accessible, meaningful, and proudly rooted in culture.

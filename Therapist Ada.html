THERAPIST ADA

Ada specializes in skincare and facial therapies, focusing on sensitive, acne-prone, and aging skin types. Her treatments blend natural ingredients—like aloe vera, turmeric, and moringa—with advanced techniques such as LED light therapy, dermaplaning, and lymphatic massage. Ada's goal is not just to treat the skin, but to help clients feel more confident and radiant in their everyday lives.

She hails from Enugu, Nigeria, where she grew up surrounded by nature and homegrown herbal remedies. Her passion for skincare began in her teenage years when she battled severe acne and found healing through traditional methods passed down from her grandmother. Determined to turn her experience into empowerment for others, Ada pursued a degree in skin therapy and later trained under renowned estheticians both locally and abroad.

With over 7 years of professional experience, Ada is known for her gentle approach, tailored routines, and deep understanding of African skin needs. She creates a soothing atmosphere for every client, ensuring that each session addresses both physical skin concerns and emotional well-being.

Outside her spa practice, Ada leads skincare workshops in rural communities, promoting skin health through natural living. She enjoys gardening, journaling, and experimenting with homemade beauty recipes. For Ada, skincare is not just cosmetic—it’s a form of healing, self-respect, and self-love.

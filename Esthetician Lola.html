ESTHETICIAN LOLA

Lola is a licensed esthetician and nail care expert. With a keen eye for detail and style, she transforms every manicure and pedicure session into a luxurious spa experience. Hailing from Surulere, Lagos State, Nigeria, Lola grew up in a vibrant, close-knit community that nurtured her creative flair from a young age. Inspired by the colorful street fashion and beauty trends of Lagos, she began practicing nail art and skincare treatments for family and neighbors while still in secondary school.

Raised by a single mother who ran a small tailoring business, Lola learned early on the value of hard work and entrepreneurship. Her passion for beauty eventually led her to pursue formal training in esthetics, where she earned multiple certifications in skincare, nail technology, and spa therapy. Today, she blends traditional Nigerian self-care wisdom with modern spa techniques to create treatments that are both healing and glamorous.

Outside of her professional work, Lola enjoys attending beauty expos, making DIY organic skincare products, and mentoring young women interested in beauty careers. Her clients describe her as warm, attentive, and highly professional—someone who not only enhances physical appearance but also boosts self-esteem and confidence. Whether you're seeking a soothing foot soak after a long day or a dazzling nail makeover for a special occasion, Lola delivers an unforgettable experience infused with passion and cultural pride.

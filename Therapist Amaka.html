THERAPIST AMAKA

Amaka is an expert in Deep Tissue therapy, known for her firm yet highly intuitive technique. Her sessions are especially effective for relieving chronic muscle tension, sports-related soreness, and long-term postural stress. With a deep understanding of muscle anatomy, she tailors each massage to target knots and deep layers of tissue, making her therapy both restorative and energizing.

She is originally from Awka, Anambra State, Nigeria. Raised in a family of healthcare workers, Amaka developed a passion for physical wellness and natural healing from a young age. After studying physical therapy in university, she trained in advanced massage techniques both in Lagos and abroad, refining her skills to meet international wellness standards.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience, Amaka has worked with athletes, office professionals, and individuals recovering from physical injuries. Her calm presence and professional confidence give her clients reassurance and comfort, turning each session into a personalized healing journey.

Outside the spa, Amaka is an advocate for women’s wellness and body positivity. She volunteers in rural outreach programs, teaching posture correction and stress-relief stretches to local communities. In her free time, she enjoys early morning jogging, reading anatomy books, and preparing healthy, traditional Igbo meals.

GROOMER MIKE

Mike offers high-end hand and foot care services specifically tailored for men, combining precision with luxury. From executive nail grooming to deep exfoliation and moisturizing treatments, he ensures every client walks out feeling clean, confident, and completely refreshed. His attention to detail and gentle approach have made him a top choice for business professionals and style-conscious men.

Born and raised in Ibadan, Oyo State, Mike grew up in a household where neatness and presentation were highly valued. Influenced by his mother, a seasoned hairdresser, he developed an early appreciation for personal care and wellness. Despite financial struggles, Mike pushed himself through vocational training in cosmetology and later specialized in men’s nail and foot care—an area often overlooked in traditional grooming.

With over 10 years of experience, Mike has built a solid reputation for professionalism and hygiene. He frequently updates his techniques through international masterclasses and proudly integrates African herbal foot soaks into his treatments. Clients admire his calm personality, discreet service, and commitment to helping men embrace self-care without stigma.

Outside of his studio, Mike enjoys jazz music, cycling around the city, and mentoring young people interested in grooming and spa therapy. To him, male grooming is not a luxury but a necessity—one that supports mental clarity, body confidence, and personal pride.
